About me

Jesimon Barreto is a Data Scientist and Computer Vision researcher with experience across R&D and industry, focusing on visual foundation models, self-supervised learning, video understanding, multimodal time series, and MLOps at scale. He has collaborated with Samsung, Petrobras, C&A, Google, iFood and Quero Delivery, covering the full ML lifecycle from data curation to deployment. He holds a BSc in Systems Engineering and an MSc in Computer Science (UFMG), the latter supported by a Google Research Scholarship.
